Meatloaf

Description

This easy meatloaf recipe is a family favorite and is super easy to make. Serve with a side of your choice for the perfect comfort meal.

The meatloaf has a flavorful zesty topping that caramelizes and sticks to the meatloaf as it bakes.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 medium onion, diced
  • 1 teaspoon butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 3/4 cup italian or seasoned breadcrumbs
  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 1 tablesppon ketchup or chili sauce
  • 1 teaspoon italian seasoning
  • 2 tablespoons parsley
  • salt & pepper
  • 1/2 cup chili sauce
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Spray a foil lined baking tray with cooking spray and set aside.
  2. In a small pan, cook onions in butter over medium low heat until tender. Let cool completely.
  3. In a medium bowl combine eggs, milk, and breadcrumbs. Allow mixture to sit for 5-10 minutes.
  4. Add the ground beef, onions, 1 tablespoon of ketchup or chili sauce, italian seasoning, parsley, and salt & pepper to taste. Mix until combined.
  5. Form and 8" X 4" loaf on the prepared baking tray. Bake for 40 minutes.
  6. Combine 1/2 cup chili sauce, 1/2 cup ketchup, and 2 tablespoons brown sugar. Spread mixture over the meatloaf and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes.
  7. Ensure meatloaf reaches 160 degrees F. Broil 1-2 minutes if desired.
  8. Allow meatloaf to rest for 10 minutes before slicing.
