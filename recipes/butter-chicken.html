Butter Chicken

Description

Butter chicken is one of the most popular curries at any Indian restaurant around the world. Beautiful, bold flavors, are what make this dish so great.

This recipe is a healthy take on the delicious dish that is so popular. Best served with Naan bread or rice for a healthier option.

Ingredients

  • 600g Diced Chicken Breast
  • 2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoon pepper
  • 15g ginger
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 140g low fat greek yogurt
  • 20g light butter
  • 1/2 sliced onion
  • 15g cashew nut butter
  • 400g diced tomatoes
  • 20ml light cream
  • 2 teaspoon coriander

Steps

  1. Mix diced chicken, 1 teaspoon of turmeric, smoked paprika, garam masala, salt, pepper, and ginger, garlic, and 100g yogurt.
  2. Cook chicken mix on high heat until almost fully cooked. Set aside.
  3. In a pan add butter, onion, diced tomatoes, cashew nut butter, 40g yogurt, and 1 teaspoon of turmeric, smoked paprika, garam masala, salt, and pepper. Mix together.
  4. Blend sauce for about 1 minute.
  5. Add the sauce back to the pan on low heat and add chicken mix. Mix together and let simmer for 3-5 minutes.
  6. Add light cream and coriander as garnish.
