Korean Beef

Description

This meal is a quick version of a classic Asian dinner served over rice. This quick and easy meal can be made in 15 minutes and tastes delicious!

The flavors of this Korean Beef blend together beautifuly with a slight sweetness and a slight bit of spice. Its a mouth-watering recipe that you must try!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup brown rice
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups chopped spinach
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon sriracha
  • 3 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 teaspoon sesame seeds

Steps

  1. In a large saucepan cook rice according to package instructions; set aside.
  2. Place eggs in a large saucepan and cover with cold water by 1 inch.
  3. Bring to a boil and cover. Boil eggs for 12 minutes over medium-high heat.
  4. Put eggs in icebath and allow to cool before peeling and slicing in half.
  5. Heat 2 tablespoons olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add 2 cloves garlic and cook 1-2 minutes, stirring frequently.
  6. Stir in spinach and cook until wilted(about 2-3 minutes). Set aside.
  7. In a small bowl, whisk together brown sugar,soy sauce, ginger, sesame oil, and sriracha.
  8. Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil in a large sillet over medium high heat. Add 2 cloves garlic and cook about 1 minute.
  9. Add ground beef and cook about 3-5 minutes or until browned, making sure to crumble the beef while cooking. Drain excess fat
  10. Stir in soy sacue mixture and green onions, and allow to simmer about 2 minutes.
  11. Serve rice, eggs, spinach, and ground beef. Garnish with green onions and sesame seeds.
